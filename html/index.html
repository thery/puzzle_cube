How does it work

  • To enter a starting position, click on the square you want to turn blue, after entering the 6 squares, chose the initial position of the cube;
  • To move the cube, just click on the square you are aiming at;
  • The black area gives some information: the number of moves that have been played so far, one optimal next move and the minimal number of moves that are necessary to complete the challenge.
  • Clicking on the number of moves performs an undo.
  • Clicking on the black area modifies the amount of information that is provided.
